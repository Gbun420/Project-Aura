## 2. Privacy Policy (GDPR & Maltese Data Protection Act)

### 2.1 Data Controller & Officer
**Aura Jobs** is the Data Controller.
**Data Protection Officer (DPO):** [aurajobs@proton.me](mailto:aurajobs@proton.me)

### 2.2 Purpose of Processing
We process personal data specifically for:
1.  **Recruitment Matching:** Identifying suitable candidates for Maltese employment vacancies using neural matching algorithms.
2.  **Compliance Verification:** Validating TCN eligibility, Skills Pass status, and PDC certification as required by Identità.
3.  **Regulatory Reporting:** maintaining audit trails for DIER and employment license requirements.

### 2.3 Third Country National (TCN) Data Handling
For TCN candidates, we process specific additional categories of data required for work permits:
- Passport validity and country of origin.
- Skills Pass / MQF Level verification.
- Pre-Departure Course (PDC) certification status.
- Health screening clearance status.

### 2.4 Data Retention Periods
We adhere to Maltese employment law retention schedules:
- **Active Candidates:** Retained for 2 years from last activity.
- **Hired Candidates:** Employment records retained for 5 years (audit requirement).
- **Unsuccessful Applicants:** Deleted after 6 months unless consent is renewed.
- **Financial/Transaction Logs:** Retained for 10 years (VAT/Tax compliance).

### 2.5 Cross-Border Data Transfers
Aura OS hosts data within the EU (Frankfurt/Dublin). Any transfer of data to non-EU/EEA countries is protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and strict encryption protocols (AES-256).

### 2.6 User Rights
Under GDPR and the Maltese Data Protection Act, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure:** Request the "Right to be Forgotten" (subject to regulatory retention overrides).
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection:** Opt-out of automated decision-making (neural matching).

To exercise these rights, contact [aurajobs@proton.me](mailto:aurajobs@proton.me).
