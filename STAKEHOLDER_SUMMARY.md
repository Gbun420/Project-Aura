# Stakeholder Summary: Aura OS V1.0

## 1. Executive Overview
Aura OS is a high-performance talent infrastructure designed to solve the 2026 Maltese recruitment crisis. By integrating hardened compliance logic with a professional, low-friction interface, Aura automates the entire lifecycle of international and local hiring.

## 2. Core Value Pillars

### **A. Revenue Protection (Bounty Guardian™)**
- **Cryptographic Handshake:** Every candidate introduction is signed with a SHA-256 HMAC, creating an immutable legal link between the agency and the employer.
- **Atomic Release:** Candidate documentation (PII) is only released upon the fulfillment of the success fee, eliminating "fee skipping."

### **B. 2026 Compliance Shield**
- **DIER-Standard Audit Trail:** Every major action—from handshake to document release—is logged in a tamper-proof audit ledger, ready for regulatory inspection.
- **PDC Monitoring:** Real-time tracking of Pre-Departure Course (PDC) validity ensures all hires meet the latest March 2026 requirements.

### **C. Efficiency & Automation (Ghost-Link™)**
- **One-Click Portal Filling:** The Aura browser extension auto-populates the Identità portal fields in seconds, reducing administrative overhead by up to 85%.
- **Neural Screening:** AI-mediated "Success Pilot" identifies high-intent candidates and triggers hiring workflows without manual intervention.

## 3. The "Standard Professional" Experience
- **Utility-First UI:** A clean, "Business White" dashboard designed for 8-hour scanability, modeled after industry-standard tools like Slack and LinkedIn.
- **Normalized Messaging:** Secure communication channels that feel familiar to users while maintaining high-integrity data encryption.

## 4. Market Readiness
- **Status:** V1.0 Prototype Finalized.
- **Infrastructure:** Hardened Node.js/SQLite core with Vite-powered professional frontend.
- **Sync:** Fully calibrated for the 2026 Maltese regulatory environment (MGA/Identità/DIER).
