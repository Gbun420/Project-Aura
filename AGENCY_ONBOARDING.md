# Aura OS: Agency Onboarding Guide (V1.0)

Welcome to the 2026 Sovereign Talent Infrastructure. This guide will walk you through the core workflows of Aura OS to ensure 100% compliance and revenue protection for your agency.

## 1. Secure Authentication

- **Access:** Log in via [https://aura-os-v1.vercel.app](https://aura-os-v1.vercel.app).
- **Agency ID:** Your unique ID is automatically linked to your Supabase session. Every introduction you make is cryptographically signed to this ID.

## 2. Monitoring the "Hiring Pulse"

- **The Dashboard:** Your main view is the **Hiring Pipeline**.
- **The Compliance Clock:** Pay close attention to candidates marked **CRITICAL**. This means their 42-day Pre-Departure Course (PDC) certificate is nearing expiry. Aura prioritizes these candidates to prevent permit delays.

## 3. The Sovereign Handshake (Bounty Guardian™)

- **Initiation:** When you find a match, use the **Aura Chat** to communicate with the candidate.
- **The Smart Trigger:** Once hiring intent is detected, the **"Unlock Paperwork"** button will glow.
- **Legal Proof:** Clicking this button logs a SHA-256 HMAC signature in the global ledger. This is your "Proof of Introduction" for DIER compliance and success fee protection.

## 4. Unlocking the "Golden Manifest"

- **Atomic Release:** Once the success fee is confirmed, the candidate's **Golden Manifest** (full PII, Passport, and PDC data) is released to your secure side-drawer.
- **Zero-Trust:** This data is only accessible to you. Aura OS staff cannot view candidate documentation.

## 5. Automated Portal Sync (Ghost-Link™)

- **Extension:** Ensure the **Aura Ghost-Link Chrome Extension** is active.
- **The Magic:** Navigate to the Identità portal (`eforms.identita.gov.mt`).
- **One-Click:** Click **"Fill Portal"** in your Aura dashboard. The extension will auto-populate all 40+ fields in seconds, including the PDC reference and housing lease IDs.
