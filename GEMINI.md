# Project Aura: Modern Talent Infrastructure

## 1. Project Mission

"Aura" is a high-performance, business-first recruitment platform for the 2026 Maltese market. It prioritizes "Seamless Utility" with deep localization for international talent and multi-sector skill mapping. It simplifies the 2026 permit crisis through automated compliance and direct operator handshakes.
